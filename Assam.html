Umananda temple
the Umananda temple, located on the Peacock Island, is one of the important temples of Guwahati. Situated in the middle of River Brahmaputra, this temple is dedicated to the worship of Lord Shiva.

Kaziranga park
Kaziranga National Park in Assam, India is famous for its one-horned rhinoceroses. It's also home to tigers, elephants, and other wildlife. The park is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Iskcon
International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), commonly referred to as the Hare Krishna movement, is a Gaudiya Vaishnava Hindu religious organization.

Majuli
Majuli, the world's largest river island, is famous for its vibrant Neo-Vaishnavite culture, rich biodiversity, stunning natural beauty, and being the first island district in India.

Kakochang falls
A Hidden Gem in Assam. KakoChang Waterfall is a stunning natural wonder tucked away in Assam, India. People often call it Kakojan Falls. This beautiful waterfall is a must-see for anyone who loves nature.
