KARNATAKA

Mysore Palace
Mysore (now Mysuru) is famous for its rich cultural heritage, majestic palaces like the Mysore Palace, vibrant festivals like Dasara, and its status as the "City of Palaces" and "Cultural Capital of Karnataka," also known for its sandalwood and silk industries.

Botanical Garden
Such a garden is important because: It is a place where a variety of endangered flora are conserved. It helps in taxonomic study and research. It provides the public with information about local and exotic species of plants.

Chikmangalur
A hill station at the foothills of the Mullayanagiri peak in the Western Ghats in Karnataka, Chikmagalur entices with trekking trails, coffee plantations, rivers, waterfalls, temples and wildlife.

Hampi
a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the former capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, renowned for its impressive ruins of ancient temples, palaces, and other structures, showcasing the grandeur of the Vijayanagara architecture.

Nandi Hills
Nandi Hills boasts a rich history that dates back to the 11th century. The hill fortress served as a strategic stronghold for various dynasties.
