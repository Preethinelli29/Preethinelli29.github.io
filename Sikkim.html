SIKKIM

Gangtok
It has an amazing view of mount Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain peak in the world. Also like everything around it, Gangtok is abundant in natural beauty and has various natural attractions.

Tsomgo Lake
According to local folklore, Tsomgo Lake holds a sacred place in the hearts of the indigenous Sikkimese people, who believe it to be the abode of protective deities.

Pelling
Pelling, a town in West Sikkim, India, is renowned for its stunning views of the Kanchenjunga mountain range, rich cultural heritage, and serene environment, making it a popular tourist destination.

Lachung
Lachung, a picturesque village nestled in the heart of Sikkim, offers travellers a serene escape amidst the majestic Himalayas. Surrounded by lush green valleys, sparkling waterfalls.

Nathula
Since it is one of the three open trading border posts between India and China, Nathu La Pass has played a key role in the Sino-Indian Trade.
