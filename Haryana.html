HARYANA

Faridabad
Faridabad is known as a major industrial hub in Haryana, famous for henna production, and for housing the Nahar Singh Stadium, a prominent cricket stadium.

Brahma Sarovar
Brahma Sarovar in Kurukshetra, Haryana, is famous for its sacred waters, believed to have been created by Lord Brahma, and its association with the creation of the universe and the Bhagavad Gita.

Karnal
Karnal, known as the "rice bowl of India," is famous for its agricultural strength, particularly its production of rice, wheat, and milk, and is also home to important agricultural research institutes.

Panchkula
Panchkula is famous for its Mansa Devi Temple, Pinjore Gardens (Yadvinder Gardens), and its proximity to Chandigarh and other popular tourist destinations.

Kurukshetra
Kurukshetra, the land where the epic battle of Mahabhrata took place, is an important pilgrim site and a holy land. The place is also where the Krishna preached Bhagawad Gita to Arjuna and sage Manu wrote Manusmriti.
