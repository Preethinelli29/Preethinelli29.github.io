TAMIL NADU

Ooty
Ooty is famous for its incredible hill views,Located at the crossroads of the states of Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka, this section of the Nilgiris range brings joy to tourists from all over the country.

Pondicherry
Pondicherry is famous for being a very old colonial town and next to the beautiful sea. The place is a wonderful specimen of French and Indian art in one place.

Kanyakumari
Kanyakumari is a popular tourist destination and pilgrimage centre in India.tourist spots include its unique sunrise and sunset points, the 41-metre (133 ft) Thiruvalluvar Statue, and Vivekananda Rock Memorial off the coast.

Velankanni
Considered to be one of the largest caves in India, the caves have karstic limestone structures, which extend to a depth of 80 m.

Thanjavur
Thanjavur is also known as Thanjai. This city was also known as Tanjore in the olden times and is an important centre of southern Indian religion, art, and architecture. It is renowned for its spectacular temples, intricate art and classical music.
