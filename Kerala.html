KERALA

VarkalaBeach
Varkala Beach, also known as Papanasam Beach, is a popular coastal destination in Kerala, India, famous for its golden sands, dramatic cliffs, and serene atmosphere.

Wayanad
Wayanad, a hill station in Kerala, is renowned for its natural beauty, including lush greenery, spice plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, and ancient caves like Edakkal.

Athirapilly waterfall
Athiramppally Falls is the largest waterfall in Kerala and is nicknamed "The Niagara of South India". Controversy about a state-proposed hydroelectric dam on the Chalakudy River.

Munnar
Virgin forests, savannah, rolling hills, scenic valleys, numerous streams, huge splashy waterfalls, sprawling tea plantations and winding walkways are all part of the great holiday experience on offer for a traveler to Munnar.

Alleppey
Most popular for its incredible backwaters, the city of Alleppey in Kerala is also a place to experience beaches, temples and traditional boat races.
