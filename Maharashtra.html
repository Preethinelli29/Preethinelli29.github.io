MAHARASHTRA

Shirdi
It is famous for being the home of the renowned 19th-century saint Shirdi Sai Baba, Devotees have a deep faith that burning incense at the Gurusthan.

Lonavala
Lonavala is famed for its breathtaking natural beauty. The landscape is dotted with emerald-green valleys, cascading waterfalls, and dense forests.

Gateway of India
Ajanta caves famous for their exquisite ancient rock-cut architecture, stunning paintings, and intricate sculptures, particularly depicting scenes from the life of Buddha and Jataka tales.

Ajanta Caves
Considered to be one of the largest caves in India, the caves have karstic limestone structures, which extend to a depth of 80 m.

Mahabaleshwar
Old Mahabaleshwar is a place of pilgrimage with its Mahadeo temple. The temple is the source of the five rivers Krishna, Koyna, Venna, Savitri and Gayatri.
