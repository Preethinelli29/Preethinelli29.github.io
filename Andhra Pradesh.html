ANDHRA PRADESH

Rushikonda Beach
Rushikonda Beach, located near Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh, India, is a popular destination known for its golden sands, gentle waves, and picturesque shoreline.

Tirupati
Tirupati is famous as a major Hindu pilgrimage site, particularly for its Tirumala Venkateswara Temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (a form of Vishnu), attracting millions of devotees seeking blessings.

Araku Valley
It is often referred to as the Ooty of Andhra Pradesh because of the beauty of the places to visit in Araku Valley and the weather. This valley in the Eastern Ghats is known for its coffee plantations.

Borra caves
Considered to be one of the largest caves in India, the caves have karstic limestone structures, which extend to a depth of 80 m.

Maredumilli
The beautiful Maredumilli waterfalls attract thousands of visitors to the region. The most famous Maredumilli waterfalls are Rajatarangini Falls, Amruthadhara Falls, and Rampa Falls.
