GOA

Dudhsagar waterfalls
Dudhsagar Falls, meaning “sea of milk” in Konkani, is a breathtaking four-level waterfall that falls down from a height of 310 meters. The sight of water flowing down from such a height into the surrounding forest creates a view that attracts tourists.

Basilica of bom jesus
This church is India's first minor basilica,The basilica contains a large number of tombs of popes and other notable people, many of which are considered outstanding artworks.

Baga Beach
Baga Beach in North Goa is a vibrant, popular beach known for its lively atmosphere, golden sands, beach shacks, water sports, and bustling nightlife.

Harvalem waterfall
The history of Arvalem Falls, also known as Harvalem Falls, dates back to the 6th century. This is believed to be the place where the Rajput mercenaries were settled by the British colonial government.

Immaculate conception church
This church houses the ancient bell that was removed from the Augustinian ruins of the Church of Our Lady of Grace. This bell is considered to be the second largest of its kind in Goa.
