GUJARAT

State of unity
The Statue of Unity is a 600-foot tall monument that depicts the Indian freedom fighter, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. He was one of the founding fathers of the Republic of India and spent his life fighting for India's independence.

Somnath temple
The site of Somnath has been a pilgrimage site from ancient times on account of being a Triveni Sangam: the confluence of three rivers namely Kapila, Hiran and Saraswati.

Kutch white desert
he park is renowned for the white sands, white sand rock formations, and the introduction to the Great Sand Sea. The larger Farafra depression covers an area of 380 square miles (980 sq km) creating the second-largest depression in Egypt.

Vadodara
Formerly known as Baroda, Vadodara was one of India's richest princely states and accumulated its wealth from trading in diamonds, cotton etc. Today, it is known for its architecture, classical music and financial superiority.

Ahmedabad
Ahmedabad is one of the biggest exporters of jewellery and gems in India and also the largest among denim suppliers in the country.
