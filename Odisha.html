ODISHA

Konark sun temple
The Konark Sun Temple, located in Odisha, India, is a 13th-century Hindu temple dedicated to the Sun God Surya, conceived as a colossal chariot with immense wheels.

Puri
Puri is famous for the world famous Shri Jagannath Temple & Longest Golden Beach. It is one of the Dhamas (Holiest of the holy place) out of four Dhamas i.e. Puri, Dwarika, Badrinath & Rameswar, in India.

Chilika lake
Chilika Lake, the largest brackish water lagoon in Asia, is famous for being a crucial wintering ground for migratory birds, particularly flamingos.

Bhubaneswar
Bhubaneswar is renowned for its exquisite temple architecture, characterized by towering spires, intricately carved sculptures, and sacred shrines dedicated to various Hindu deities.

Rourkela
