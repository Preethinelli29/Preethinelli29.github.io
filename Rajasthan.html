RAJASTHAN

Hawa Mahal
Hawa Mahal, or the "Palace of Winds," is special for its unique honeycomb-like facade with numerous small windows (jharokhas) that allowed royal women to observe street life.

Ranthambore National Park
Ranthambore National Park is known for its high number of tigers, thus, the chances of sighting a tiger is quite high.

Udaipur
Besides lakes, Udaipur is also known for its historic forts and palaces, museums, galleries, natural locations and gardens, architectural temples, as well as traditional fairs, festivals and structures.

Jaisalmer
This 'Golden City' is located close to the Pakistan border and in close proximity to the Thar Desert. The city's most prominent landmark is the Jaisalmer Fort, also called Sonar Qila (Golden Fort).

Mehrangarh Fort
Mehrangarh Fort, located in Jodhpur, is famous for its historical significance, impressive architecture, and the Mehrangarh Museum which houses priceless relics of Indian courtly life.
